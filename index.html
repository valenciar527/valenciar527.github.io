About Me

Welcome to my portfolio website! I am currently a masters student for Instructional Design at Florida State University just about to graduate! My graduation goals are to start my career in the corporate sector of instructional design, and hone in my craft by becoming experienced at all aspects of the instructional design process. So far, my instructional design experience has being focused on design and development, including learning how to use new technologies and incorporating my front-end web development skills. When I am not talking about instructional design, I like to play music (drums and a little piano), make pixel art, and doing voiceovers.

On this website, you will find the classes I took for my master's program, some artifacts that demonstrate my competencies as an instructional designer, my resume, and methods to contact me. Most of the listed artifacts demonstrate my designing abilities, writing and communication abilities, and my interpersonal skills. I plan to add to my portfolio with future experience and projects!

I’m hard at work preparing to join the working world in instructional design, and I am eager to make genuine great works with others, learn a thing or two along the way, and make the places I go better than I found them.

I hope you enjoy this portfolio, and look forward to hear from you!

Articulate Storyline 360

Basics to Music
Basics of Music
Basics of Music - Details
Context

This class assignment had us follow the whole instructional design process using the ADDIE model. I used Articulate 360 to create the lesson, implementing the work prior to determine the design.

Conditions

This was the culmination of a semester's work. Prior to this, we conducted front-end analysis of the topic, audience and context, deconstructed the topic's subordinate skills and entry behavior, and created assessment and evaluation methods.

Scope

This was the first time I used Storyline 360, which I utilized the variable, triggers, and quiz pool functionality. I also created vector assets using Adobe Illustrator for the music symbols, as well as provided the voiceover.

Role

Solo work; completed analysis, design and development.

Displayed Competencies
  • Analysis Skills
    • Analyze learning and performance problems to recommend appropriate solutions.
    • Determine subordinate and prerequisite knowledge and skills.
  • Design Skills
    • Apply learning theory and systems thinking to design practice.
    • Identify the scope and sequence for instructional solutions.
    • Use visual design principles appropriately.
    • Align outcomes, strategies, and assessments.
    • Generate appropriate instructional strategies and activities.
  • Technology and Media Skills
    • Develop instructional materials using a variety of media (interactive activities, audio-visual).
    • Develop and use web-based instruction/e-learning tools.
